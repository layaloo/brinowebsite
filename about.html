Inbound marketing: earning attention before asking for anything

When we started Brino, we did not have a huge audience or an established name. We had our experience, our ideas, and the determination to make useful work visible.

Our inbound process begins by listening. We note the questions founders ask us about websites, SEO, branding, and growth. Then we turn those real questions into helpful landing pages, articles, case studies, and social posts. The goal is simple: give someone clarity before they ever book a call.

What this looks like week to week

  • Research the problems and search terms our ideal clients actually use.
  • Create practical content that answers one specific question well.
  • Connect each article to a relevant service and a natural next step.
  • Review visits, enquiries, and conversations to learn what resonates.

We are learning in public. Some ideas attract attention quickly; others teach us what to improve. That honesty is part of the process. Sustainable inbound marketing is not one viral post—it is a useful library that earns trust over time.

Outbound marketing: starting genuine conversations

A startup cannot always wait to be discovered. Outbound marketing helps us meet the right people sooner—but we never want it to feel like noise.

Before reaching out, we learn about the business. We look at its positioning, website, customer journey, and visible growth opportunities. If we believe we can genuinely help, we send a short personal message explaining what we noticed and why it may matter.

Our human-first outreach process

  • Choose a small, relevant group instead of sending messages to everyone.
  • Research each company and identify a specific opportunity.
  • Write like a person, not an automated sales script.
  • Offer a useful observation, audit, or idea before requesting a meeting.
  • Follow up respectfully and stop when the timing is not right.

For us, outbound is not about forcing a sale. It is about opening a door. Every reply—even a “not now”—helps us understand the market and become better founders, marketers, and partners.
