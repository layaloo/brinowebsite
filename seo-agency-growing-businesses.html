Frequently asked questions

How quickly does SEO work?

Some technical improvements can produce early changes, but competitive organic growth usually requires consistent work over several months.

Is SEO better than paid advertising?

They serve different purposes. SEO creates longer-term organic visibility, while paid advertising can generate immediate exposure. Many businesses benefit from both.

Does Brino guarantee first position on Google?

No credible agency can guarantee a specific organic ranking. We set measurable objectives related to visibility, qualified traffic, enquiries, and conversions.

Can Brino create SEO landing pages?

Yes. We research keywords and develop focused pages that answer searchers’ questions and guide them toward the appropriate service.
